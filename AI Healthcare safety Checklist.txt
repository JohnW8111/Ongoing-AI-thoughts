AI Healthcare Safety Checklist ( need to think about systems as you think about individual items)

1. **Bias and Discrimination**
   - [ ] Assess for biases in AI algorithms.
   - [ ] Check for discriminatory or exclusionary outputs.
   - [ ] Evaluate performance across different demographic groups.

2. **Data Privacy and Security**
   - [ ] Ensure protection against privacy leaks.
   - [ ] Guard against potential attacks and vulnerabilities.
   - [ ] Check for unintended bugs and their consequences.
   - [ ] Implement strict user authentication and access controls.

3. **Information Quality**
   - [ ] Monitor for disinformation or malicious use.
   - [ ] Assess risks of misinformation.
   - [ ] Evaluate logical consistency of AI outputs.
   - [ ] Verify the necessity of human oversight.
   - [ ] Assess for overstated AI abilities.

4. **Social and Economic Impacts**
   - [ ] Consider impacts on creative economies and job markets.
   - [ ] Evaluate the computational cost and environmental impact.

5. **Research and Academic Implications**
   - [ ] Address authorship issues in AI-assisted research.
   - [ ] Ensure research integrity and transparency.
   - [ ] Assess the focus on diverse AI research areas.

6. **Human-Computer Interaction Concerns**
   - [ ] Check for over-reliance on AI.
   - [ ] Assess interaction harms and stereotype perpetuation.
   - [ ] Identify vulnerable user groups.

7. **Regulation and Accountability**
   - [ ] Ensure accountability mechanisms for AI errors.
   - [ ] Check for regulatory compliance.
   - [ ] Consider implications on human rights.

8. **Clinical Validation and Accuracy**
   - [ ] Confirm the AI has been clinically validated for its intended use.
   - [ ] Assess the accuracy and reliability of AI predictions.
   - [ ] Compare AI recommendations with current clinical guidelines.

9. **User Training and Competency**
   - [ ] Ensure healthcare professionals are adequately trained to use the AI tool.
   - [ ] Assess the level of AI understanding among staff.
   - [ ] Provide continuous education and updates on AI tool changes.

10. **Integration with Healthcare Systems**
    - [ ] Ensure seamless integration with existing healthcare IT systems.
    - [ ] Evaluate the impact of AI on existing workflows and processes.

11. **Patient Consent and Transparency**
    - [ ] Inform patients about the use of AI in their care.
    - [ ] Obtain necessary consents for the use of AI tools.

12. **Emergency and Fallback Procedures**
    - [ ] Establish protocols for system failures or unexpected AI behavior.
    - [ ] Ensure availability of manual processes in case of AI system malfunction.

13. **Post-Deployment Surveillance**
    - [ ] Monitor for long-term effects and unforeseen consequences.
    - [ ] Regularly update the AI system with new data and insights.

### Additional Considerations
- **Collaboration with AI Developers:** Maintain communication with AI developers for ongoing support and updates.
- **Patient Involvement:** Engage patients in discussions about AI, addressing their concerns and expectations.
